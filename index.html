About Us



Welcome to Elephant's Crest Lodge, where luxury meets adventure amidst the breathtaking landscapes of Victoria Falls, Zimbabwe.
Nestled just 1.5 kilometers from the renowned Victoria Falls Game Reserve, our lodge invites you to embark on a journey of
unparalleled safari experiences.
At Elephant's Crest Lodge, we pride ourselves on offering discerning travelers the epitome of luxury accommodation coupled with
exhilarating wildlife encounters. Imagine waking up to the calls of the wild and stepping onto your private veranda to witness elephants
majestically roaming nearby, as the African sun paints the sky in hues of orange and gold.
Our lodge provides a sanctuary of comfort and elegance, where every detail has been meticulously crafted to ensure your stay exceeds
expectations. Indulge in spacious and lavishly appointed rooms, each offering panoramic views of the surrounding wilderness. Unwind in
style amidst modern amenities while immersing yourself in the sights and sounds of the African bush.
Venture out on guided safari excursions led by our experienced rangers, as you traverse the untamed terrain in search of Africa's iconic
Big Five and other mesmerizing wildlife species. Whether it's a thrilling game drive, a serene bush walk, or a captivating sunset river
cruise, every moment promises to be filled with awe and wonder.
Beyond the safari, Elephant's Crest Lodge offers a myriad of unforgettable experiences, from cultural encounters with local communities to
adrenaline-pumping activities such as bungee jumping or helicopter rides over the majestic Victoria Falls.
Come and experience the ultimate blend of luxury and adventure at Elephant's Crest Lodge, where every day brings new discoveries and
unforgettable memories in the heart of Zimbabwe's wilderness. Your safari adventure of a lifetime awaits.
