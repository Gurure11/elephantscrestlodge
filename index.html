About Us

Welcome to Elephant's Crest Lodge, where luxury meets adventure amidst the breathtaking landscapes of Victoria Falls, Zimbabwe. Nestled just 1.5 kilometers from the renowned Victoria Falls Game Reserve, our lodge invites you to embark on a journey of unparalleled safari experiences.

Imagine waking up to the calls of the wild and stepping onto your private veranda to witness elephants majestically roaming nearby, as the African sun paints the sky in hues of orange and gold. Every detail has been meticulously crafted to ensure your stay exceeds expectations.

Guided safari excursions • Big Five • Bush walks • Sunset river cruises • Helicopter tours
